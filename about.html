About

Tia-Lana Chinapyel. I make images. Sometimes I capture them, sometimes I invent them. Photographer in the morning, art director in the afternoon — and the other way around the next day.

I work between portrait, editorial and film posters. I like brands that have something to say and faces that don't lie. It all starts with a notebook, a location scout, an idea jotted down too fast.
