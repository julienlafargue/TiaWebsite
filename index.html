About Me

A creative with a story to tell

My name is Tia-Lana and I'm a UK-born creative based in Paris. I have a deep love for visual storytelling — exploring emotion, narrative and aesthetics through photography and acting.

Whether behind the lens or in front of it, I bring the same care for light, atmosphere and human connection to every project.
